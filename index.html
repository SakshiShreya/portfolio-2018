The camera sends real-time videos to Raspberry Pi through wifi which acts as a local server. This video can be accessed on any smartphone on the IP address of Raspberry Pi. The smartphone has an app "Wireless IMU" to send the Accelerometer and Magnetometer readings to the Raspberry Pi. Raspberry Pi controls the movement of servo motors based on these readings.
